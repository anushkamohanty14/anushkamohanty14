## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🎯 [TrueHire — AI-Powered Career Intelligence Platform](https://web-production-b1dbc.up.railway.app)
End-to-end career matching platform built and shipped as a team. Combines cognitive ability assessment, NLP resume parsing, and hybrid job matching across 894 O*NET job titles.

- **Hybrid engine:** 3-signal scoring (cosine similarity, dot-product, Jaccard) across O*NET occupational data  
- **NLP pipeline:** Rule-based resume parser extracting 130+ skills, education, certifications from PDF/DOCX  
- **Cognitive scoring:** Accuracy + reaction-time composites mapped to NCPT population percentiles via empirical CDF  

`Python` `FastAPI` `Scikit-learn` `SciPy` `MongoDB Atlas` `Railway`

---

### 📡 [Fake News Spread Simulator](https://fakenewsspreadsimulator-1.onrender.com/)
Agent-based simulation of misinformation diffusion across network topologies — research submitted to COMSNETS 2026 & ICDCN 2026.

- **Five-state probabilistic diffusion model** with non-Markovian forwarding-limit mechanism  
- **Topology-dependent findings:** forwarding limits cut peak misinformation by 26.5pp in random networks; combined intervention achieved 52.3pp reduction  
- Validated across 50-run Monte Carlo; deployed live  

`Python` `FastAPI` `NetworkX` `Agent-Based Modelling` `vis-network.js`

---

### 🩺 [Multimodal Skin Disease Detection](https://github.com/anushkamohanty14/Multimodal-Skin-Disease-Detection)
Fine-tuned **PubMedBERT** (clinical metadata) + **Swin Transformer** (dermoscopic images), fused via a gated fusion network for skin lesion classification on HAM10000.  
**Result:** 91% accuracy, 0.817 macro F1 — outperforming both unimodal baselines  

`Python` `Transformers` `Swin` `PubMedBERT` `Keras` `Healthcare AI`
